MLSListings Inc is the premier Multiple Listing Service (MLS), for northern California. MLSListings is where real estate property listings originate, as the authorized trading platform for real estate professionals. Their customers are REALTORS®, brokers and agents throughout northern California. Approximately 16,000 real estate professionals in over 6,000 firms representing 28,000 square miles conduct business utilizing their MLS platform. Real estate data is the most up-to-date, refreshing every five minutes.

Located in the heart of Silicon Vallery, real estate agents now have a mobile solution for their tech savy agents allowing them to collaborate with their clients while out and about. DDoS was a major challenge degrading user-experience from the mobile app.

Apakau’s API DDN reduced the latency of MLS APIs to less than 200 milliseconds across the US — more than 10 times faster than what the company has been able to deliver using traditional means. A total of 31,060 API requests were processed during the test period using Apakau's API DDN compared to 3265 requests by going directly to the origin servers. This results in a seamless and more engaging user experience without adding costly infrastructure or network bandwidth.
