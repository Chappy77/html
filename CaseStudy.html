HolidayIQ.com improved its site performance, site availability by 100% and boosted website traffic growth using Akamai’s Web Experience Solution

HolidayIQ.com, is India’s first and largest holiday information portal powered by India’s first and largest traveller community. Its purpose is to unleash the power of user opinions to create a smarter Indian traveller. HolidayIQ.com has about 30000 Indian hotels & 1300 Indian destinations listed on the website, which is used by 50 Lac Indian travellers every month.

For HolidayIQ.com it was extremely important to have a website that did not load the origin server, increase website performance for end user transactions, manage traffic growth to the site more effectively and provide a more enjoyable online experience for its users. The site had been experiencing exponential traffic growth which was affecting its performance. The HolidayIQ.com team felt the need to support growing traffic volumes and address unpredictable traffic spikes while providing users with a fast and more engaging experience in order to retain their brand value and leadership position.
